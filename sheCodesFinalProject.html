Welcome to Beautifully Crowned !

Psalms 139:14 (KJV) - "I will praise thee; for I am fearfully and wonderfully made: Marvellous are thy works; And that my soul knoweth right well."

Historically, natural hair in the Black community has faced societal challenges, with different types of curls or coils often drawing varying forms of attention.

When I chose to stop chemically straightening my hair in 2017, I made the decision to embrace the hair that was naturally mine. Embracing my tightly coiled strands wasn't always easy, as it didn't align with society's standard of beauty.

However, as I learned to nurture and appreciate my hair, I developed a newfound confidence. I came to understand that my crown is beautiful, and I am fearfully and wonderfully made.
